About us

Welcome to Soaporium, your number one source for all things soap. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of handmade soaps, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by a passionate group of soap enthusiasts, Soaporium has come a long way from its beginnings in a small studio. When we first started out, our love for natural, artisanal products drove us to start our own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
